Gourmet Seafood Paella

Overview

This exquisite dish combines the freshest seafood with aromatic saffron-infused rice, creating a delightful culinary experience.

Ingredients

The main ingredients include:

  • Arborio rice
  • Fresh shrimp
  • Mussels
  • Calamari
  • Saffron
  • Bell peppers
  • Garlic
  • White wine
  • Vegetable broth

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan.
  2. Saut√© garlic and bell peppers until soft.
  3. Add rice and stir to coat in oil.
  4. Pour in white wine and let it evaporate.
  5. Add broth and saffron, then simmer.
  6. Incorporate seafood and cook until done.
Serving Suggestions

Serve the paella hot, garnished with fresh parsley and lemon wedges. Pair it with a chilled white wine for a perfect meal.

Nutritional Information

This dish is rich in protein and omega-3 fatty acids, making it a healthy choice for seafood lovers. One serving contains approximately:

  • Calories: 450
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 15g
